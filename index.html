About The Crochet Circle

Crochet Circle is more than just a crafting community – we're a passionate group of individuals who believe in the power of creativity, connection, and handmade traditions. Founded in 2000 by a group of friends who shared a love for crochet, our mission is to inspire, support, and bring together crochet enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn your first stitch or an experienced crocheter seeking to share your latest project, Crochet Circle is your home for all things yarn and hooks.

Our Community Values:

  • Creativity and Self-Expression
  • Inclusive Learning
  • Sustainable Crafting Practices
